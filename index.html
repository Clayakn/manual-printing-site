Frequently asked questions

How fast can you produce manuals?

Standard production is 4-5 business days. Rush orders can be completed in as little as 48 hours after proof approval.

Can you help with design or layout?

Yes. Our design specialists can clean up files, adjust bleeds, and add branded elements. Hourly creative services start at $95.

Do you offer fulfillment to multiple addresses?

Absolutely. Provide your address list and we can split shipments with individual tracking numbers for each location.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept PDF, InDesign packages, or PowerPoint files. We provide preflight checks on every order.
